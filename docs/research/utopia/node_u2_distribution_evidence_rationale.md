## TL;DR

Node U2 asks: **P(AI productivity gains are broadly shared)** via SWF, compute dividend, tax/competition mix, augmentation policy — not merely created. **Modal (~55–65%): Path D** — Ghost GDP dynamics, compute-owner bifurcation, patchwork labor rules, **no** federal UBI/SWF by 2032. **Success window opens @C4** (Block-class cuts, Canaries −16%, Challenger 101k AI-cited YTD) — same trigger as doom Node 1, but **success branch** = redistribution coalition + institutional absorption, not pause. **Primary competitor to U3/U4:** concentrated harm (P **0.68–0.78** that top-decile capture ≥60% of AI-attributed surplus through C8). **Top cruxes:** (1) Ghost GDP without transfer enactment; (2) so-so automation > machine usefulness; (3) SWF rhetoric >> SWF law; (4) Global South rule-taker status; (5) UBI solves income not Jahoda latent functions.

**Bucket mapping:** U3/U4 **primary**; U2 symbiosis **blocked** if distribution fails even when alignment succeeds (Path E).
