## 0. Node definition & U2 gate

**Question:** Conditional on alignment success and material abundance (U2 *physical* path open), what is **P(institutions sustain eudaimonia when AI makes human labor non-instrumental)**?

**Distinction (Bostrom):**
- **Post-work / shallow redundancy:** AI does tasks better; humans can still play chess, craft, care, govern — recreational or dignity-preserving niches remain.
- **Post-instrumental / deep redundancy:** AI does tasks better *and* understands/appreciates them better; human effort is redundant for *practical and evaluative* purposes.

**U2 normative gate:** U2 = symbiotic flourishing = aligned superintelligence **plus** humans retaining meaningful agency + eudaimonia. U7 is the **normative** bottleneck: U2 physical (abundance, safety, augmentation) can succeed while U7 fails → **Path family E: aligned-but-bored** (whimper channel; blocks U2/U3 narratively).

**Not this node's job:** Distribution (U2 economic node), alignment (U1), science dividend (U3). U7 assumes those can succeed and asks whether life is *worth living* at scale.

## Evidence sections

### P = 0.72 — Post-work latent-function deprivation is real [EST]

**Claim:** Unemployment/retirement without structured substitutes predicts psychological distress **independently of income** in ~**72%** of comparable studies (directional consensus; magnitude varies).

**Why:** Jahoda (1982) five latent functions — time structure, social contact, collective purpose, status, activity — plus manifest income. Meta-analysis (Paul & Batinic; 113 studies): latent + manifest functions explain ~**19%** of mental-health variance; deprivation correlates with distress **more strongly than financial difficulty alone** in many samples.

**Evidence:**
- [Paul & Batinic meta-analysis, Frontiers 2023](https://www.frontiersin.org/journals/psychology/articles/10.3389/fpsyg.2023.1017358/full) — **[EST]**
- `<!-- private monorepo only -->` §4; `thread5_human_meaning_ubi.md` §2.2

**Analogue:** Marienthal unemployed (1930s Austria) — collapse of daily rhythm and social role, not only wallet.

**Would update if:** Large panel shows income replacement alone closes ≥80% of unemployment mental-health gap with no structural interventions.

**Conf:** H

### P = 0.55 — UBI satisfies manifest but not latent functions [EST]

**Claim:** In modal UBI RCT designs, **~55%** probability that cash transfers address income without closing Jahoda/SDT gaps (autonomy/competence/relatedness via non-work channels).

**Why:** Finland: health gains track income more than condition removal; OpenResearch 2024: leisure↑, family care↑, but small/non-sustained mental-health gains, labor hours↓; Alaska PFD: no participation collapse but **too small** for post-work test. SDT: work is primary adult vehicle for all three needs (`values_psychology_map.md` §3).

**Evidence:**

**Analogue:** Lottery winners — life satisfaction↑, day-to-day happiness flat (Lindqvist et al., RESTUD 2020).

**Would update if:** Long-duration UBI (≥$30k/yr equiv, ≥8yr) shows latent-function parity vs employment on validated scales.

## 3. Falsifiers (node-level)

1. **Population eudaimonia** (WHO-5, Ryff, or pre-registered composite) **≥ pre-AI baseline** in highest-AI-adoption decile for **10 consecutive years** post-C8.
2. **Civic participation** (volunteering, association membership, local governance attendance) **↑ ≥25%** from 2024 baseline in same regions — not only online engagement.
3. **FIRE return-to-work** rate falls **<15%** among voluntary post-work cohorts with strong social infrastructure — suggests institutions work.
4. **Human-only cultural/economic sectors** stable **≥10% employment share** without subsidy after AI superhuman in those domains.
5. **Binding treaty** on paideutic AI design with **third-party audit** and documented skill/moral deliberation metrics — compliance verified.
