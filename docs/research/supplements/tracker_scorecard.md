## 0.70× calendar lag rule

Capability and governance milestones in the private AI 2027 scenario calendar are mapped to **~0.70× wall-clock** when anchoring sim events to observed 2026–2028 data:

| Track | AI 2027 calendar | Observed / projected (2026-06) | Lag |
|-------|------------------|--------------------------------|-----|
| METR time horizon | C8–C9 beats | **Ahead** of 2027 script on coding/agents | ~30% faster |
| Automated R&D / RSI metrics | C9–C10 | Mixed Ahead/Behind | ~0.70× on governance |
| Agent capability (Agent-2/4 class) | 2027 H2 | Emerging 2028 Q1–Q2 at C5+ | ~0.70× |
| Federal governance (pause, GAAIA) | 2027 | **Emerging** not enacted | slow track +30% |

**Use:** When a research file cites a 2027 calendar month, multiply by **~0.70** for capability-anchored dates unless the row is explicitly on the governance slow track (+30% politics lag). Whistleblower and regulatory forks follow **capability anchor**, not fixed October 2027.
